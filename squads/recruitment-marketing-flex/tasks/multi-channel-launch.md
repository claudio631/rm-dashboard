# Multi-Channel Campaign Launch

Coordinate a synchronized recruitment marketing campaign across all Indeed Flex advertising channels.

## Launch Coordination

All squad members participate:
- **@ppc-paid-media-specialist** — Leads launch, manages paid channels
- **@seo-content-strategist** — Ensures landing pages and organic content are ready
- **@crm-email-specialist** — Activates supporting email/SMS sequences
- **@analytics-performance-lead** — Sets up tracking and monitoring dashboards
- **@ai-automation-specialist** — Configures automation rules and smart bidding

## Budget Allocation Framework

| Channel | Typical % | Best For |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Indeed Ads | 30-40% | Direct job applicants |
| Google Ads | 20-30% | High-intent job seekers |
| Meta Ads | 15-20% | Passive candidate awareness |
| Bing Ads | 5-10% | Incremental reach, lower CPC |
| Reddit/Craigslist | 5-10% | Local, gig-economy audience |
| Programmatic | 0-10% | Scale and automation |
