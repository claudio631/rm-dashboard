# SEO Job Posting Optimization

Optimize Indeed Flex job postings for maximum organic search visibility and Google for Jobs inclusion.

## Key Optimization Areas

1. **Job Title** — Match high-volume search terms while being descriptive
2. **Description** — Structured, scannable, keyword-rich
3. **Schema Markup** — Google for Jobs compliant JobPosting schema
4. **Location** — City + state for local search targeting
5. **Salary** — Include pay range (ranking factor for Google for Jobs)

## Best Practices for Indeed Flex

- Use "flexible" and "temp" naturally in job descriptions
- Highlight shift flexibility as a key differentiator
- Include "no experience required" where applicable (high search volume)
- Optimize for "[job type] near me" search patterns
- Add structured FAQ section for common candidate questions
