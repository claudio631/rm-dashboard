# Task: Google Ads Keyword Research

> Agent: @ppc-paid-media-specialist
> Elicit: true
> Dependencies: keyword-strategy-guide.md

## Objective
Conduct comprehensive keyword research for Google Ads campaigns targeting candidate acquisition.

## Pre-Conditions
- Job categories and markets defined
- Google Ads account with Keyword Planner access
- Understanding of target candidate profiles

## Workflow

### Step 1: Define Research Scope
**Elicit:** Which job categories and markets?
- [ ] List target job titles/roles
- [ ] List target geographic markets
- [ ] Define candidate intent level (active job seeker vs. passive)
- [ ] Identify competitor employers to research

### Step 2: Seed Keyword Generation
- [ ] Brainstorm core terms per job category:
  ```
  {Job Title} + jobs/work/hiring/employment
  {Job Title} + {City}/near me
  {Job Title} + {Qualifier: pay, hours, temp, flexible}
  ```
- [ ] Pull terms from existing job postings
- [ ] Review Search Terms Reports from active campaigns
- [ ] Analyze competitor ad copy for keyword ideas
- [ ] Check industry job boards for common terminology

### Step 3: Keyword Planner Research
- [ ] Enter seed keywords into Keyword Planner
- [ ] Filter by target locations
- [ ] Export keyword ideas with:
  - Monthly search volume
  - Competition level
  - Suggested bid range
  - Trend data (seasonality)
- [ ] Identify high-volume, low-competition opportunities
- [ ] Note seasonal patterns for hiring-related terms

### Step 4: Keyword Categorization
Organize keywords into themes:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| Job title specific | "warehouse worker jobs", "forklift operator hiring" |
| Location-modified | "jobs in Houston", "Dallas temp work" |
| Benefit-driven | "same day pay jobs", "flexible schedule work" |
| Intent signals | "apply for jobs", "hiring near me today" |
| Industry terms | "staffing agency", "temp agency near me" |
| Long-tail | "warehouse night shift jobs $18 hour Dallas" |

### Step 5: Competitive Analysis
- [ ] Identify top 5 competitor employers in target markets
- [ ] Research their keyword strategies (Auction Insights)
- [ ] Note gaps where competitors are absent
- [ ] Identify expensive keywords to potentially avoid
- [ ] Find unique terms competitors aren't targeting

### Step 6: Negative Keyword Development
- [ ] Create master negative keyword list:
  - Irrelevant intent (salary, reviews, interview questions)
  - Wrong job types (if not offered)
  - Competitor brand names (unless targeting)
  - Geographic exclusions
  - Qualification mismatches
- [ ] Organize negatives at campaign and ad group levels

### Step 7: Ad Group Structure
- [ ] Group keywords by theme (one theme per ad group)
- [ ] Limit to 3-20 keywords per ad group
- [ ] Assign match types based on strategy:
  - Smart Bidding: Broad match primary
  - Manual bidding: Mix of phrase and exact
- [ ] Map each ad group to a specific landing page
- [ ] Document keyword-to-ad-group mapping

### Step 8: Volume & Budget Estimation
- [ ] Estimate total monthly clicks per campaign
- [ ] Calculate required budget (clicks × avg CPC)
- [ ] Compare against available budget
- [ ] Prioritize keywords by ROI potential
- [ ] Create phased rollout plan if budget-constrained

## Post-Conditions
- Comprehensive keyword list organized by ad group
- Negative keyword lists created
- Budget estimates documented
- Keyword-to-landing-page mapping complete

## Outputs
- Keyword research spreadsheet
- Ad group structure document
- Negative keyword lists
- Budget estimation
