# Google Ads AI & Automation Guide

> Source: [Google Business Resource Hub](https://business.google.com/us/resources/)

## Overview

Google Ads increasingly uses AI to optimize campaigns. Understanding and leveraging these features is critical for maximizing performance.

---

## AI-Powered Features

### Smart Bidding
Automated bid strategies that use machine learning to optimize for conversions in every auction.

| Strategy | Goal | Requirements |
|----------|------|-------------|
| Maximize Conversions | Most conversions within budget | 15+ conversions/30 days |
| Target CPA | Specific cost per conversion | 30+ conversions/30 days |
| Maximize Conversion Value | Highest total conversion value | Conversion values set |
| Target ROAS | Specific return on ad spend | 50+ conversions/30 days |

**Key stat:** Switching from Target CPA to Target ROAS yields **+14% more conversion value** at similar ROAS.

### AI Max for Search
Activating AI Max in Search campaigns:
- Auto-generates text assets from landing pages
- Expands keyword coverage beyond your lists
- **+14% more conversions** at similar CPA (average)
- Text guidelines give creative control over AI suggestions

### Performance Max (AI-Native)
Fully AI-driven campaign type:
- Serves across all Google channels
- Automatically optimizes creative combinations
- Uses audience signals to find ideal candidates
- **+18% incremental conversions** vs. manual campaigns

### Responsive Search Ads
AI assembles your headline/description combinations:
- Provide 15 headlines + 4 descriptions
- AI tests thousands of combinations
- Serves best-performing combination per search query
- Ad Strength indicator guides optimization

### Responsive Display Ads
AI creates display ads from your assets:
- Auto-adjusts size and format per placement
- Tests image + text combinations
- Serves video when it outperforms static images
- Reaches across 2M+ sites and apps

## Foundational Requirements for AI Success

### 1. Sitewide Google Tag
- Install on ALL pages, not just conversion pages
- Enables Smart Bidding to understand user journey
- Use Google Tag Manager for easy management

### 2. Enhanced Conversions
- Securely hash first-party data (email, phone)
- Recovers conversions lost to cookie limitations
- Critical for accurate AI optimization signals

### 3. First-Party Data (Customer Match)
- Upload candidate/worker email lists
- AI finds similar high-value candidates
- Improves audience signal quality for PMax
- Requires consent-based data collection

### 4. Strong Creative Assets
- More assets = more combinations for AI to test
- Higher quality inputs = better AI outputs
- Refresh regularly to prevent creative fatigue

### 5. Google Analytics 4
- Deeper behavioral insights
- Import GA4 goals as conversion actions
- Understand full candidate journey
- Audience building for remarketing

## AI Optimization Best Practices

### Do's
1. **Provide abundant inputs** — more headlines, images, and audience signals = better AI performance
2. **Use broad match + Smart Bidding** — gives AI maximum signal access
3. **Set conversion values** — enables value-based optimization
4. **Allow learning periods** — 2-6 weeks before evaluating AI performance
5. **Review AI insights** — check Insights page for audience/search theme data
6. **Experiment regularly** — use Google Ads experiments to test AI features

### Don'ts
1. **Don't change settings during learning** — resets AI optimization
2. **Don't limit AI with overly narrow targeting** — broad inputs perform better
3. **Don't ignore AI recommendations** — evaluate them, but don't dismiss automatically
4. **Don't set unrealistic targets** — CPA/ROAS targets should be achievable
5. **Don't skip negative keywords** — AI still needs guidance on what to exclude
6. **Don't rely solely on AI for creative** — human insight + AI optimization is the winning formula

## Organizational Mindset for AI

Google recommends building a **"culture of experimentation"**:

| Old Approach | AI-First Approach |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Manual bid management | Smart Bidding with value signals |
| Exact match keywords only | Broad match + AI optimization |
| Manual ad creation | Responsive ads with abundant assets |
| Channel-specific campaigns | Performance Max cross-channel |
| Weekly manual optimization | AI-driven with human oversight |
| Siloed budgets by channel | Flexible budgets following performance |

### Transition Steps
1. Start with Smart Bidding on existing campaigns
2. Expand match types from exact/phrase to broad
3. Upgrade to responsive search ads (provide max assets)
4. Add Performance Max alongside Search
5. Enable enhanced conversions and Customer Match
6. Review and adjust monthly — let AI do daily optimization
