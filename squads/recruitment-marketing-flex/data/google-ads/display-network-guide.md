# Google Display Network Guide

> Source: [Google Business Resource Hub](https://business.google.com/us/resources/)

## Overview

The Google Display Network (GDN) reaches **2 million+ websites, videos, and apps** — covering nearly all global internet users. Use it for awareness, remarketing, and reaching passive candidates beyond Search.

---

## When to Use Display

| Scenario | Priority |
|----------|----------|
| Remarketing to site visitors who didn't apply | High |
| Building employer brand awareness | Medium |
| Reaching passive candidates | Medium |
| Supplementing Search campaigns | Medium |
| Testing new market awareness | Low-Medium |

**Important:** Display has lower conversion rates than Search (0.5-2% vs. 3-8%). Use it primarily for remarketing and awareness, not as your primary application driver.

## Targeting Methods

### Interest-Based Targeting

#### Affinity Audiences
80+ predefined interest groups. Relevant for recruitment:
- Job Seekers
- Business Professionals
- Auto Enthusiasts (for driving roles)
- Health & Fitness (for physical roles)
- Home & Garden (for maintenance roles)

#### Custom Affinity Audiences
Create your own audiences using:
- **Keywords:** "warehouse jobs", "temp work near me"
- **URLs:** Competitor career pages, job boards
- **Apps:** Job search apps
- **Combinations:** Keywords + URLs for refined targeting

#### In-Market Audiences
People actively researching or comparing:
- Employment Services
- Job Listings
- Staffing Agencies
- Education & Training

### Demographic Targeting
| Dimension | Options | Recruitment Use |
|-----------|---------|-----------------|
| Age | 18-24, 25-34, 35-44, 45-54, 55-64, 65+ | Working-age filter |
| Gender | Male, Female, Unknown | Role-relevant targeting |
| Parental Status | Parent, Not a parent | Schedule-sensitive roles |
| Household Income | Top 10% to Lower 50% | Pay-range alignment |

### Remarketing
| List Type | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Site Visitors | Anyone who visited your site | All career page visitors |
| Page-Specific | Visitors to specific pages | Job listing page visitors |
| Converters | People who already converted | Exclude from acquisition |
| Non-Converters | Visited but didn't apply | Re-engage with application CTA |
| Time-Based | Visited in last X days | 7-day, 14-day, 30-day lists |

### Contextual Targeting
| Method | How It Works | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Topics | Match to content categories | Jobs & Education |
| Placements | Specific websites/apps | Job boards, news sites |
| Keywords | Content containing keywords | Pages mentioning "hiring" |

## Responsive Display Ads

### Why Use Them
- **Auto-adapt** to any ad space (size, shape, format)
- **One creation** = millions of placements
- **AI optimization** tests combinations automatically
- **Video integration** — serves video when it outperforms images

### Asset Requirements
| Asset | Specification | Count |
|-------|--------------|-------|
| Short headline | 25 characters | 1-5 |
| Long headline | 90 characters | 1 |
| Description | 90 characters | 1-5 |
| Business name | 25 characters | 1 |
| Landscape image | 1200×628 px | 1-15 |
| Square image | 1200×1200 px | 1-15 |
| Logo (square) | 1200×1200 px | 1-5 |
| Logo (landscape) | 1200×300 px | 0-5 |
| Video | YouTube URL | 0-5 |

### Creative Best Practices
1. Use high-quality workplace photos (real, not stock)
2. Include diverse talent in images
3. Show the actual job environment
4. Keep text overlay minimal (Google's AI adds your text)
5. Use brand colors in images for recognition
6. Create both lifestyle and logo-focused images
7. Upload video assets — they often outperform static images

## Combining Targeting Methods

### Recommended Combinations

#### High Priority: Remarketing
```
Audience: Site visitors (last 30 days) who didn't apply
Exclusion: People who already applied
Creative: "Still interested? Apply now — positions filling fast"
Bid adjustment: +50% over base
```

#### Medium Priority: Custom Intent
```
Audience: Custom segment (job search keywords + competitor URLs)
Demographics: Age 18-54
Creative: Job-specific ads with pay and location
Bid adjustment: Base
```

#### Lower Priority: Broad Awareness
```
Audience: Affinity (Job Seekers) + In-Market (Employment)
Demographics: Working age
Geographic: Markets with open positions
Creative: Employer brand messaging
Bid adjustment: -20% from base
```

### Combining Demographics + Interest
Layering demographics on interest audiences creates **narrower, more relevant** targeting. But be careful:
- Too narrow = low volume
- Test before committing budget
- Start broad, then narrow based on performance data

## Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Review placement report — exclude low-quality sites
- [ ] Check audience performance — adjust bids per segment
- [ ] Refresh creative assets monthly
- [ ] Review frequency cap — avoid ad fatigue (3-5 impressions/user/day)
- [ ] Exclude converted users from acquisition campaigns
- [ ] Monitor viewability metrics (target >60%)
- [ ] Test new audience combinations quarterly
