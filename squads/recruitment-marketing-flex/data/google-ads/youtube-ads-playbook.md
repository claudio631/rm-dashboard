# YouTube Ads Playbook

> Source: [Google Business Resource Hub](https://business.google.com/us/resources/)

## Why YouTube for Recruitment?

YouTube is the world's second-largest search engine. Candidates use it for:
- Company culture research
- Job role previews ("day in the life" videos)
- Industry information
- Skills tutorials

**Key stat:** BlendJet achieved **413% revenue growth** using YouTube strategy. Cider reduced **CPA by 33%** with YouTube Shorts.

## Ad Formats

### Skippable In-Stream Ads
- Play before, during, or after videos
- Viewers can skip after 5 seconds
- **Pay:** Only when someone watches 30s or interacts (TrueView)
- **Best for:** Employer brand storytelling, culture videos
- **Recommended length:** 15-60 seconds

### Non-Skippable In-Stream Ads
- 15-30 seconds, cannot be skipped
- **Pay:** Per impression (CPM)
- **Best for:** Key messages that must be fully seen
- **Use when:** Short, high-impact message (e.g., hiring event)

### Bumper Ads
- 6 seconds maximum, non-skippable
- **Pay:** Per impression (CPM)
- **Best for:** Quick brand impressions, reinforcement
- **Use for:** "We're Hiring!" awareness blitzes

### In-Feed Video Ads
- Appear in YouTube search results and browse feed
- Users choose to watch (click to play)
- **Pay:** When user clicks to watch
- **Best for:** Longer content, testimonials, "about us" videos

### YouTube Shorts Ads
- Appear between Shorts content
- Skippable, vertical format
- **Best for:** Mobile-first audiences, younger demographics
- **Format:** Under 60 seconds, vertical (9:16)

### Masthead Ads
- Premium placement on YouTube Home feed
- **Pay:** CPM or CPD (cost per day)
- **Best for:** Major hiring events, market launches
- **Note:** Requires Google sales rep, high budget

## ABCDs of Effective Video Ads

Google's framework for video creative:

### A — Attract
- Hook viewers in the first 5 seconds
- Use bold visuals, motion, and sound
- Show people immediately (faces drive engagement)
- Start with the most compelling frame

### B — Brand
- Introduce your employer brand early (within first 5 seconds)
- Show logo and company name
- Use brand colors and visual identity
- Mention company name in voiceover

### C — Connect
- Tell a story that resonates with your audience
- Feature real employees/workers
- Show the actual work environment
- Address candidate pain points (flexibility, pay, growth)

### D — Direct
- End with a clear call-to-action
- "Apply Now", "Sign Up Today", "Visit [URL]"
- Include end screen with clickable CTA button
- Make next step obvious and easy

## Targeting Strategies

### Audience Targeting
| Method | Description | Recruitment Use |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Demographics | Age, gender, parental status, income | Target working-age candidates |
| Interests | Affinity audiences (80+ categories) | Career-minded, job-seeking interests |
| In-Market | Actively researching similar offerings | Active job seekers |
| Custom Segments | Your keywords + competitor URLs | Target people searching job terms |
| Remarketing | Previous website visitors | Re-engage candidates who didn't apply |
| Similar Audiences | Lookalikes of your converters | Find more candidates like your best |

### Contextual Targeting
| Method | Description | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Topics | YouTube content categories | Jobs & Education, Business |
| Placements | Specific channels/videos | Career advice channels |
| Keywords | Content matching keywords | Videos about "finding a job" |

### Geographic & Schedule
- Target specific cities/markets with open positions
- Schedule ads for peak viewing times (evenings, weekends)
- Adjust bids by location based on application rates

## Bidding Strategies

| Strategy | Pay Per | Best For |
|----------|---------|----------|
| CPV (Cost-per-view) | 30s view or interaction | Engagement, consideration |
| Target CPM | 1,000 impressions | Awareness, reach |
| Target CPA | Conversion | Direct response (applications) |
| Maximize Conversions | Conversion | Volume focus |

### Budget Recommendations
- Start small: $20-50/day per campaign
- Scale based on performance after 2-4 weeks
- Allocate 70% to proven formats, 30% to testing

## Creative Best Practices for Recruitment Videos

### Content Ideas
1. **"Day in the Life"** — Follow a worker through their shift
2. **Employee Testimonials** — Real workers sharing their experience
3. **Culture Showcase** — Team environment, workplace culture
4. **How to Apply** — Walk through the application process
5. **Benefits Highlight** — Pay, flexibility, growth opportunities
6. **Hiring Event Promo** — Date, location, what to expect

### Technical Specs
| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| Resolution | 1080p minimum (1920x1080) |
| Aspect Ratio | 16:9 (landscape) or 9:16 (Shorts) |
| File Format | MP4, MOV, AVI |
| Max Length | No limit for in-stream; 6s for bumpers |
| Audio | Clear voiceover or music; captions required |
| Captions | Required (many watch without sound) |

### Production Tips
- Film horizontally AND vertically (use for both standard + Shorts)
- Include captions/subtitles for all spoken content
- Keep branding consistent across all videos
- Feature diverse talent reflecting your workforce
- Shoot in real locations, not studios (authenticity matters)

## Measurement

### Key Metrics
| Metric | What It Measures |
|--------|-----------------|
| View Rate | % of people who watched vs. saw your ad |
| CPV | Average cost per view |
| View-Through Conversions | Conversions after ad view (no click) |
| Click-Through Rate | % who clicked your CTA |
| Watch Time | Average seconds viewed |
| Brand Lift | Surveys measuring awareness/consideration shift |

### Reporting Cadence
- **Weekly:** Views, CPV, conversion trends
- **Monthly:** Full performance review, creative refresh
- **Quarterly:** Strategy assessment, format mix optimization
