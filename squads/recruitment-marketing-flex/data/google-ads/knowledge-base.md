## Recruitment Marketing Context

When applying these guides to recruitment campaigns:
- **"Product"** = job opportunity / employer brand
- **"Customer"** = candidate / job seeker
- **"Conversion"** = application submitted / registration completed
- **"Landing page"** = job listing page / application form
- **"Purchase"** = completed onboarding / first shift worked
- Focus keywords on job titles, locations, and shift types
- Geo-target to markets with active hiring needs
- Use ad scheduling aligned with candidate search patterns (evenings, weekends)
