## Recruiter Feedback

- Almost every flexer expected an interview at the event (we didn't have team available for that)
- Address change likely caused lower attendance
- Need Indeed Flex signage at future events
- Google Sheet check-in worked well — standardize for all events
- Only ~2 people came specifically for DTs — we emailed 500
- Privacy was an issue — some flexers preferred their car or stepping outside for the interview

## Key Takeaways

1. **Show rate improved from 41% (Mar 11) to 55% (Mar 16)** — second event benefited from learnings
2. **DT pass rate near-perfect** (93-98%) — workers who show up are ready
3. **Stord dominated preference** (34 of 46 arrivals on Mar 16) — aligns with Stord's stronger downstream conversion in LV
4. **DT-only marketing doesn't work** — 500 emails sent, only 2 came for DT
5. **AI interview expectation gap** is the #1 friction — must require completion before event

## What to Fix for Next Event

- [ ] Require AI interview completion before RSVP confirmation
- [ ] Lock venue address 2+ weeks in advance, never change
- [ ] Order Indeed Flex signage/banners
- [ ] Pre-build Google Sheet check-in (standardize template)
- [ ] Book private rooms for any on-site interviews
- [ ] Provide company phones for I-9 verification
- [ ] Don't market as "DT event" — it's a full onboarding acceleration event
