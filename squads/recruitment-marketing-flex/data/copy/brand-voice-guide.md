# For use by Quill (Copywriter Agent) across all candidate-facing copy
# Last Updated: 2026-04-02

## Brand Positioning

**What Indeed Flex is:**
A flexible workforce platform that connects workers with shifts at top employers — giving candidates control over when, where, and how much they work.

**Core candidate value proposition:**
> "Work on your terms. Choose your shifts. Get paid fast."

**Differentiation from traditional staffing:**
- No long-term commitment required
- Candidate controls their own schedule
- Same-day pay removes financial waiting
- Access to premium employers (stadiums, hotels, corporate events)
- Free upskilling builds long-term career value

---

## Voice Pillars

### 1. Empowering
The candidate is in control. Copy positions Indeed Flex as the enabler, not the employer dictating terms.

- ✅ "Choose shifts that fit your life"
- ✅ "You decide when you work"
- ❌ "We need you available on call"
- ❌ "Must be flexible to employer needs"

### 2. Energetic (Not Aggressive)
Enthusiastic about opportunities without sounding desperate or pushy.

- ✅ "Exciting events, premium venues — real opportunities"
- ✅ "Join a team that works hard and gets rewarded for it"
- ❌ "AMAZING OPPORTUNITY!!! Don't miss out!!!"
- ❌ "Apply RIGHT NOW before it's too late"

### 3. Clear and Direct
No jargon, no corporate-speak, no fluff. Say what it is.

- ✅ "$21/hr. Pick your shifts. Get paid the same day."
- ✅ "You'll load and unload event equipment. It's physical work and we pay well for it."
- ❌ "Leverage your physical aptitude in a dynamic logistical environment"
- ❌ "Synergistic opportunity in the growing gig economy space"

### 4. Inclusive
Copy should never make candidates feel excluded. Avoid language that skews toward a specific gender, age, or background.

- ✅ "Server", "Line Cook", "Loader" — gender-neutral titles
- ✅ "All backgrounds welcome"
- ❌ "Waitress", "Busboy", "Hostess"
- ❌ "Young, energetic team"
- ❌ "Recent grads preferred"

### 5. Urgently Helpful (Not Pushy)
Create urgency through opportunity scarcity, not pressure tactics.

- ✅ "Shifts fill fast — apply today to secure yours"
- ✅ "Hiring now for upcoming events in [city]"
- ❌ "LAST CHANCE. Apply or lose this forever."
- ❌ "We're only hiring 3 more people — hurry"

## Approved Vocabulary

### Benefits Language
| Say This | Not This |
|----------|----------|
| Same-Day Pay / Same Day Pay | Instant pay, Real-time pay |
| Flexible shifts | Gig work, Temp work (too low-status) |
| Choose your schedule | Work when available |
| Upskilling / Free training | Mandatory training |
| Health, dental, and vision coverage | Insurance benefits |
| Premium events / Upscale venues | Random jobs |

### Action Words (CTAs)
- Apply Now
- Get Started
- Book Your Shifts
- See Open Roles
- Join the Team
- Apply in Minutes

### Avoid
- "Click here" (vague)
- "Submit" (robotic)
- "Inquire" (too formal)
- "Register" (feels like more work)

## Candidate Personas — Copy Angles

### The Schedule-Seeker
**Who:** Parent, student, or second-job holder who needs work to fit around commitments
**Lead with:** Flexibility, shift control, part-time options
**Headline formula:** "Work Around Your Life — [Job] Shifts in [City]"

### The Income-Booster
**Who:** Someone with a main job or fixed income looking for supplemental earnings
**Lead with:** Pay rate, same-day pay, quick starts
**Headline formula:** "$[X]/hr | [Job Type] Shifts | Earn More This Week"

### The Career-Starter
**Who:** No formal experience, looking for a first professional role or new industry entry
**Lead with:** Training, no-experience language, approachability
**Headline formula:** "No Experience Needed | [Job] Jobs | $[X]/hr in [City]"

### The Premium Worker
**Who:** Experienced hospitality/events worker who values the quality of venues and events
**Lead with:** Premium venues, VIP access, polished work environment
**Headline formula:** "Premium Events | [Job Title] | $[X]/hr in [City]"

### The Flex-Loyal
**Who:** Already knows Indeed Flex, looking for more shifts or new markets
**Lead with:** New events, new markets, loyalty/retention angle
**Headline formula:** "New Shifts Available | [City] | Book Now on Indeed Flex"

## Prohibited Phrases

Never use in any Indeed Flex candidate-facing copy:

| Prohibited | Reason |
|-----------|--------|
| "Competitive pay" | Meaningless without a number — EEOC adjacent |
| "Must be a team player" | Cliché with zero information value |
| "Fast-paced environment" (standalone) | Overused filler — show it, don't say it |
| "We are an equal opportunity employer" (in ad copy) | Fine in job postings footer — not in ad headlines |
| "Unlimited earning potential" | False/misleading for hourly roles |
| "Be your own boss" | Incorrect — workers are placed by Indeed Flex |
| Any salary claim without a verified number | Legal risk |

## Spanish Voice Notes

For US Hispanic audiences (primarily Texas, Florida, California, Illinois markets):

- Use **"tú"** form — informal register builds connection for hourly workers
- Keep benefit language the same — "pago el mismo día" not "pago en tiempo real"
- "Turnos flexibles" and "horarios flexibles" are both acceptable
- Avoid overly formal Portuguese-influenced Spanish — write natural spoken Mexican/Central American register
- "Cobra hoy" (Collect today) is a high-urgency, culturally resonant CTA
- "Únete al equipo" (Join the team) works well for hospitality roles
