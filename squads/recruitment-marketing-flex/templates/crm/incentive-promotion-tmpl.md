# CRM Template: Incentive & Promotion Campaign

**Case:** UK Festive Bonus — £10/shift late-night bartender/waiting staff
**Use for:** Time-limited bonus or incentive campaigns to drive shift fulfilment for specific roles
**Type:** Multi-step (Email + SMS/Push), urgency-driven, role/location specific

---

## Brief Structure

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Objective** | Drive shift bookings for [ROLE] by promoting a time-limited incentive |
| **Campaign Goal** | Book shift |
| **Primary Metric** | Shift fulfilment rate for [ROLE] |
| **Audience** | Users verified for [ROLE], [LOCATION], active in Flex |

---

## When to Use This Template

- Client or ops team needs a fulfilment boost for specific roles
- Time-limited bonus, referral payout, or promotional offer is available
- Seasonal demand surge (holidays, events, summer peaks)
- Role has historically low booking rate and needs incentive push

---

## Email Templates

### Email 1 — Announcement

**Subject:** Get your $[AMOUNT] [BONUS NAME] + so much more!
**Pre-header:** Tons of perks when you work shifts with Flex!
**Header:** [BONUS NAME] is live!

**Body:**
```
Hi [First Name],

Our [BONUS/PROMOTION NAME] is live!

Earn an extra $[AMOUNT] for every [ROLE] shift you work [CONDITION — e.g., that finishes after 11pm / on weekends / during event week].

[OPTIONAL: A nice little extra for you.]

And not only that, but there are plenty of other perks for working through Flex, including Instant Pay, referral bonuses, [OTHER PERKS].

So what's holding you back?
Have a look at what's available in your area and book your next shift today!

[CTA: Book Shift]

[SIGN-OFF — e.g., Happy shift hunting!]

Best wishes,
Team Flex
```

---

### Email 2 — Social Proof / FOMO

**Subject:** Don't miss out — claim your [BONUS NAME]!
**Pre-header:** Other workers are already getting theirs!
**Header:** Don't miss out — claim your [PROMO/GIFT]!

**Body:**
```
Other workers are getting their $[AMOUNT] [BONUS TYPE], so why not get yours?

Simply work a [ROLE] shift [CONDITION].

[OPTIONAL: Book before [DATE] to qualify.]

[CTA: Book Shift]
```

---

### Email 3 — Reminder / Mid-Campaign

**Subject:** Fellow workers are already cashing in — are you?
**Pre-header:** Your [BONUS NAME] is waiting

**Body:**
```
Hi [First Name],

Fellow workers are already getting their hands on their [BONUS NAME], so why don't you get yours?

Work a [ROLE] shift [CONDITION] and get an extra $[AMOUNT] per shift!

[CTA: Book Shift]
```


## Push Notification Template

**Header:** $[AMOUNT] bonus — book a [ROLE] shift now!
**Body:** Work [CONDITION] and earn extra. See available shifts in your area.

---

## Qualification Conditions Reference

| Condition Type | Example |
|---|---|
| Time-based | Shifts finishing after 11pm |
| Role-based | Bartender or waiting staff only |
| Date window | December 20–31 |
| Shift count | First 3 shifts after NHO |
| Location | Within [X] miles of [CITY] |
| Tier | More shifts worked = higher bonus |

---

## Placeholders Reference

| Placeholder | Description |
|---|---|
| `[ROLE]` | e.g., Bartender, Event Staff, Picker Packer |
| `[AMOUNT]` | e.g., 10, 25, 50 |
| `[BONUS NAME]` | e.g., Festive Bonus, Shift Completion Bonus |
| `[CONDITION]` | e.g., finishing after 11pm / during event week |
| `[LOCATION]` | e.g., Dallas, TX / UK |
| `[DATE]` | Campaign end date |
| `[PERKS]` | Other Flex perks to mention |
| `[LINK]` | App deep link or short URL |
| `[SIGN-OFF]` | e.g., Happy shift hunting! / Let's do this! |
