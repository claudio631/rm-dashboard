# CRM Template: Client Compliance Reengagement

**Case:** Cort Furniture — 9-panel drug test compliance (Las Vegas, NV — Dec 2025 / Mar 2026)
**Use for:** Client-specific compliance campaigns where workers need to complete a certification/test before accessing shifts
**Type:** Multi-channel (Email + SMS), Active + Inactive segments

---

## Brief Structure

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Objective** | Re-engage [MARKET] marketplace to complete [CLIENT] compliance requirement and access new shifts |
| **Campaign Goal** | Complete compliance ([COMPLIANCE TYPE]) |
| **Primary Metric** | Compliance completion rate |
| **Audience** | [CITY/MARKET] — Active + Inactive users eligible for [CLIENT] roles |

---

## When to Use This Template

- Client has specific compliance requirement (drug test, background check, certification, etc.)
- Shifts are available but workers are not yet compliant
- Targeting a specific metro market with unfilled [CLIENT] positions
- Second or subsequent round of compliance comms (reference first round dates)

---

## Audience Notes

- Pull list from Redash filtered by: market = [CITY], eligible for [CLIENT], compliance status = incomplete
- Segment into Active (recent shift/login) vs Inactive (no activity 90+ days)
- Provide audience list link in brief when submitting to CRM team

## Email Templates

### Email 1 — Inactive Users

**Subject:** Want to earn more through [CLIENT]?
**Pre-header:** Get compliant, see more shifts
**Header:** See new opportunities

**Body:**
```
Hi [First Name],

Want to start earning through Flex again?

We've got brand-new [ROLE] roles available with [CLIENT], paying up to $[RATE]/hr.

If you've completed your [COMPLIANCE TYPE], you can book any available shifts now. If you haven't, please go to the app and complete your [CLIENT] onboarding.

To get started, go to 'Browse Jobs', tap an '[SHIFT NAME]' shift, and you'll be shown how to complete [COMPLIANCE TYPE].

[CTA: Find Shifts]

Once that's complete, you can book as many [CLIENT] shifts as are available.
```

---

## SMS Templates

### SMS 1 — Active + Inactive (combined)

```
Hi [First Name], brand-new [CLIENT] shifts in your area.
[COMPLIANCE TYPE] complete? Then book.
If not, please complete your [CLIENT] onboarding in the app.

Shifts pay up to $[RATE]/hr.
Book here: [DEEP LINK]
```

### SMS 2 — Inactive Users

```
Hi [First Name], fancy booking new [CLIENT] shifts through Flex? Had your [COMPLIANCE TYPE]? Then book.
If not, please complete your [CLIENT] onboarding in the app.

Shifts pay up to $[RATE]/hr. Book here: [LINK]
```

