## Story

**As a** recruitment marketing specialist,
**I want** to see cost-per-RSVP, cost-per-verified, and cost-per-shift-completed broken down by client and market,
**so that** I know the true cost of acquiring a working Flexer through each channel and market.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Cost analysis page at `/analytics/costs` or section within dashboard
2. Calculates: Cost per Account (spend / accounts created), Cost per RSVP (spend / AI interviews), Cost per Verified (spend / platform verified), Cost per Shift Completed (spend / 1st shift completed)
3. Breakdown by client showing all cost-per-stage metrics in a sortable table
4. Breakdown by market showing same metrics
5. Channel-level cost comparison when Indeed spend data is available alongside funnel data
6. Visual chart comparing cost-per-stage across clients (bar chart)
7. Highlight anomalies: markets where cost-per-completed is >2x the average
8. Total spend and blended cost metrics shown as summary cards
9. Include incentive costs when incentive data is uploaded (adds to total cost)
