## Story

**As a** recruitment marketing specialist,
**I want** an AI-powered ad copy generator that creates recruitment ad variants for Indeed, Google, Meta, and TikTok,
**so that** I can quickly produce ad copy for new campaigns across channels.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Ad copy generator page at `/tools/ad-copy`
2. Input: job title, location, pay range, shift type, client (optional for cobranding), channel selection
3. Generates template-based variants + AI-enhanced creative options via Claude
4. Channel-specific character limits enforced (Google headlines=30, descriptions=90; Meta primary=125)
5. "Generate All Channels" produces copy for all platforms simultaneously
6. Copy-to-clipboard and CSV export
7. Cobranding toggle: when client allows, include client name in ad copy
