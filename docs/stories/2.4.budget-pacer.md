## Story

**As a** marketing team lead,
**I want** a budget pacing dashboard that shows spend progress per channel with overspend/underspend alerts,
**so that** I can ensure Indeed Flex's advertising budget is utilized efficiently without waste.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Budget pacing widget on the main dashboard (`/dashboard`) showing per-channel pacing status
2. Dedicated budget page at `/budget` with detailed multi-channel pacing view
3. Each channel shows: total budget, spent, remaining, daily target, pacing ratio, status (ON_PACE / OVER / UNDER)
4. Visual pacing indicator (progress bar with color coding: green=on pace, yellow=slightly off, red=critical)
5. Alerts triggered when pacing ratio exceeds ±25% (critical) or ±10% (warning)
6. Recommendation text for each channel: what to do to get back on pace
7. Budget allocation view: pie/bar chart showing spend distribution across channels
8. Configurable budget periods (weekly, monthly, quarterly) and date range selection
9. Summary row showing blended totals across all channels
