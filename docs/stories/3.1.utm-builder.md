## Story

**As a** recruitment marketing specialist,
**I want** an in-app UTM builder that follows our naming convention and pre-populates Indeed Flex channels,
**so that** I can generate consistent tracking URLs without the shared spreadsheet.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. UTM builder page at `/tools/utm-builder`
2. Form inputs: source (dropdown with Indeed Flex channels), medium, market, job category, objective, date, content, term
3. Real-time preview of generated UTM string and full URL
4. Batch mode: generate UTMs for multiple ad variants with different `utm_content`
5. Copy-to-clipboard for individual URLs and bulk copy
6. Validation: lowercase, hyphens only, no special characters
7. History of last 20 generated UTMs in localStorage
