> Source: Stakeholder interview — 2026-03-17

## Pay Structure

- Pay ranges are **variable** — determined by **client + location** combination
- Not fixed per job category
- Example: Warehouse Associate in Columbus, OH may differ from Warehouse Associate in Las Vegas, NV
- **Implication:** Ad copy and job postings need dynamic pay rates per market/client

## Shift Types

All roles are shift-based, booked via the Indeed Flex app:
- **Day shifts**
- **Night/overnight shifts**
- **Weekend shifts**
- **Event-based** (variable schedule, hospitality)

## Implications for PPC Manager AI

### Campaign Structure
- Campaigns should be organized by **vertical (Industrial vs Hospitality)** first, then by market
- Different ad copy, keywords, and audiences for Industrial vs Hospitality roles
- Industrial keywords: "warehouse jobs", "forklift operator", "general labor"
- Hospitality keywords: "hotel jobs", "banquet server", "event staff", "catering jobs"

### Keyword Strategy Priority
1. **Industrial high-volume:** warehouse, forklift, picker packer, general labor
2. **Hospitality high-volume:** hotel, banquet server, event staff, dishwasher, housekeeping
3. **Market-specific:** "{role} jobs {city}" combinations

### Ad Creative Strategy
- Industrial: Emphasize weekly pay, flexible scheduling, no experience needed
- Hospitality: Emphasize tips potential, event variety, schedule flexibility
- Both: "Download the Indeed Flex app" as primary CTA

### Audience Targeting
- Industrial audiences: logistics workers, Amazon warehouse workers, gig economy
- Hospitality audiences: restaurant workers, hotel staff, event workers, students
