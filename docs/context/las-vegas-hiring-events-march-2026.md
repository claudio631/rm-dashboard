## Recruiter Feedback (Marcus)

### AI Interview Scheduling Blocker (Critical)

Workers arriving at the March 16 event had **pre-scheduled AI interviews booked** via the app. This **blocked them from conducting an on-the-spot AI interview**. The workaround required each worker to cancel their scheduled interview via confirmation email, reopen the app, and select "Interview Now."

- ~12 workers waited at the start of the event before this was resolved
- Recruiting team conducted **in-person interviews** to prevent further delays
- "Almost every Flexer who came in was expecting an interview" — defeats the purpose of the AI interview (anytime convenience)

### Recommendations from Marcus

1. **Require AI interview completion before the event** — or at minimum, clearly advise workers to complete it ahead of time. If not completed, a recruiter must manually approve before onboarding can proceed.
2. **Re-evaluate event purpose** — if interviews are expected in-person, the space needs private rooms (some workers requested stepping outside or using their car for privacy).
3. **Pre-build Google Sheet check-in** — the team created one for this event, recommend standardizing for all future events.
4. **Address change notification** — venue change likely contributed to lower attendance; ensure adequate lead time for location changes.
5. **Indeed Flex signage** — no branding at the event location; add visible signage for future events.
6. **Company phone for I-9 verification** — team members doing document verification need a company number, not personal devices.
7. **DT-specific traffic was minimal** — only ~2 people came specifically for drug testing.
