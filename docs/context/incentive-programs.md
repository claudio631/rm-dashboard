# Indeed Flex — Bonus Incentive Programs

> Source: `Active Incentives (1).xlsx` — imported 2026-03-17
> Incentives are used when regular ads can't meet high demand.

## Overview

When client demand exceeds what normal advertising can deliver, the RM team deploys **bonus incentives** to motivate Flexers to complete shifts. These are tracked in a Google Sheet and managed by the 3 US RM specialists.

## Incentive Types

| Type | Count | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **5 shifts** | 14 | Complete 5 shifts to earn bonus |
| **5 consecutive shifts** | 10 | Complete 5 shifts in a row |
| **Referral** | 6 | Refer a new Flexer who completes shifts |
| **1 shift** | 3 | Complete 1 shift (low barrier, urgent need) |
| **Other** | 11 | Various: 2 shifts, 10 consecutive, prize draws, conversions |

## Incentive Structure

Each incentive record tracks:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Incentive Name | Descriptive name with client, location, dates |
| Incentive Type | Shift completion, referral, prize draw, conversion |
| Bonus Amount | Dollar amount paid to Flexer |
| Timeframe | Start/end dates or "until X workers reached" |
| Terms and Conditions | Link to Google Doc with full T&C |
| Link to Winners | Google Sheet tracking qualified Flexers |
| POC | Team member managing the incentive |
| OBS/Status | Active, completed, or notes |

## Implications for RM Team AI

### Incentives as a Marketing Lever

Incentives sit **alongside ads** as a demand-generation tool:

```
Low demand → Regular ads (Indeed, Google, Meta)
Medium demand → Ads + targeted Braze re-engagement
High demand → Ads + Braze + Bonus incentives
Critical demand → All of above + hiring events
```

### What RM Team AI Should Track

1. **Incentive cost per market** — total bonus payouts alongside ad spend
2. **True cost-per-hire including incentives** — ad spend + bonus payouts / hires
3. **Incentive effectiveness** — do incentives actually increase shift completion rates?
4. **Incentive vs ad ROI** — is a $50 bonus more cost-effective than $50 more in Indeed ads?
5. **Automated incentive triggers** — when demand exceeds capacity, suggest launching incentives

### Integration

- Track incentive spend alongside ad spend in unified budget view
- Include incentive cost in cost-per-hire calculations
- Alert when a market might need incentives (high demand, low fill rate)
- Historical incentive performance by client/market for decision support
