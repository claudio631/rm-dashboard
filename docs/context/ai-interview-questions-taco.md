> Questions marked with * are scored for the role
> Questions with [skill tags] evaluate specific competencies

## Overview

The recruitment team configures AI interview questions per role. Each question has:
- **Code**: Unique identifier (e.g., US-OE-000-01)
- **Question**: What the AI asks the job seeker
- **What is evaluated**: The competency being assessed
- **Skill**: Specific skill tag (used for matching to shifts)
- **Scored**: Whether the answer impacts the candidate's rating

Interviews are available in **English and Spanish** (job seeker chooses).

## Common Screening Questions (across all roles)

These questions appear in most/all roles as initial screening:

1. *"Can you tell me what attracted you to this role and why you believe you're a strong fit?"*
2. *"When would you like to start working?"*
3. *"Are you currently employed or applying via other agencies?"*
4. *"Are you comfortable with prolonged periods of standing, walking, lifting...?"* [Physical]
5. *"Are you able to fluently speak Spanish?"* [Bilingual]
6. *"Do you have a GED or High school Diploma?"* [Education]
7. *"How would your last supervisor describe your attendance and reliability?"* [Scored]
8. *"Can you tell me about a time you received feedback about your performance?"* [Scored]

## Industrial Question Themes

### Physical Requirements (all industrial roles)
- Lifting capacity: 55 lb standard, 75 lb for heavy roles
- Standing/walking for extended periods
- Working in varying warehouse temperatures

### Technical Skills (role-specific)
- **Forklift**: Certification type, safety protocols, load management
- **Assembler**: Hand/power tools, quality inspection, assembly instructions
- **Machine Operator**: Equipment types, setup procedures, troubleshooting
- **Repair Technician**: Diagnostic skills, tool proficiency, electrical/mechanical

### Behavioral (all roles)
- Attendance and reliability
- Handling feedback
- Working under pressure / fast-paced environments
- Teamwork and communication

## Hospitality Question Themes

### Service Skills (customer-facing roles)
- Customer interaction and conflict resolution
- POS/cash handling experience
- Multitasking during busy periods

### Kitchen Skills (BOH roles)
- Food safety and sanitation (ServSafe)
- Knife skills and food prep techniques
- Kitchen station management
- Allergen awareness

### General (all hospitality)
- Appearance and grooming standards
- Availability for nights/weekends/holidays
- Team collaboration
- Handling stressful service situations

## Implications for PPC Manager AI

1. **Interview questions define role requirements** — these map to what should be in job descriptions and ad copy
2. **Skill tags** determine which shifts a Flexer can book — important for understanding the verified → booked conversion
3. **Scored questions** are the quality gate — candidates who score poorly here get filtered out by recruiters
4. **Spanish language support** should be reflected in ad targeting (bilingual audiences)
5. **Physical requirements** (lifting 55lb, 75lb) must be in ad copy to pre-qualify candidates and reduce funnel drop-off
6. **Ad copy optimization**: Include key requirements upfront (physical demands, certifications) to attract qualified candidates and reduce interview failure rate
