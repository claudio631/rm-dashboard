How to read this dashboard — data sources, calculations & accuracy ▼

What is this dashboard?

Every row is one revenue team request (from US_Recruitment_Requests). Requests are never merged — two requests for the same client and city appear as two separate rows. Sorted oldest-to-newest by submission date.

Status badges: O Open / Live   C Complete   D Declined

Declined and Complete rows are grayed out. Their metric cells are intentionally blank — we don't track pipeline for requests we're not actively running.

FHS Requisitions (Open · Interview · Target)

Open — count of FHS requisitions with status open or auto-paused that match this request's client + location + role. A value of 0 = no live req in FHS yet (flagged in the No FHS KPI card).

Interview — sum of RSVPs (candidates who signed up for an interview) from all matching FHS reqs created after the revenue request submission date.

Target = HC × 10. Rule of thumb: 10 interview candidates needed per hire.

Matching priority: first tries client + location + role; falls back to location + role only (picks up Indeed Flex reqs for the same metro).

OB Funnel (Created · Verified · RTB)

Sourced from the OB Funnel Custom Viewer Tableau export. The funnel tracks workers in the onboarding pipeline for the last 30 rolling days.

  • Created — workers who created an account and applied
  • Verified — passed 1st role verification step
  • RTB (Ready to Book) — completed full onboarding, available for shifts

Matching uses the same client + metro logic as FHS. Values roll up the Total sub-row (all roles combined for that client/location).

Fulfillment (Target · Fill%)

RTB Target = HC × 2.5. We aim for 2.5× the headcount in Ready-to-Book workers to have buffer for no-shows and cancellations.

Fill% = RTB ÷ (HC × 2.5), capped at 100%.

Progress bar colors: red <25% · amber <50% · green ≥50%

Shifts column

Yes = shift schedules are posted in ACP for this client + location + role. No = no shift posted yet.

Shifts data is manually maintained. A "No" doesn't mean shifts won't happen — it means they haven't been entered in ACP yet.

Notes column

Free-text field you can type into directly in the browser. Notes are saved in your browser's localStorage — they persist across page refreshes on the same device but are not shared with others and will not survive clearing browser data.

Complete / Frozen rows

When a request moves to Complete, its FHS, OB Funnel, and Indeed metrics are frozen at the values recorded on completion day. This prevents historical records from being overwritten as the pipeline data rolls forward. Frozen data is stored in src/data/frozen-requests.json.

Data accuracy & what to expect

FHS Requisitions
High
Direct CSV export from FHS. Refreshed every EOD run. Match accuracy depends on consistent naming — slight variations (e.g. "Nashville, TN" vs "Nashville") may cause misses.
OB Funnel
Medium
30-day rolling window. Values represent all workers in that metro for the client — not exclusively for your request. Shared metros (e.g. CORT Chicago) aggregate all CORT Chicago workers regardless of specific event.
Indeed Campaigns
Medium
Matched by campaign name keywords (client + city + role). Spend is cumulative for the file's date range — may include prior-month campaigns still running. A missing "⚠️" does not always mean no campaign exists.
Row completeness
High
Every row in the Revenue Requests CSV appears here — no rows are dropped or merged. If you see a request in the CSV it will be here.
Shifts (ACP)
Manual
Shifts data depends on ACP being up to date. Treated as best-effort — verify directly in ACP for critical decisions.
Fill% reliability
Indicative
RTB is a leading indicator — workers in the pipeline are not guaranteed to show or complete shifts. Use Fill% to spot low-supply requests early, not as a confirmed fulfillment number.
