## Department Overview

**Mission:** Drive candidate acquisition, onboarding completion, and shift booking across 21 US markets for 147 clients through paid media, CRM, SEO, and data-driven optimization.

**Business model context:** Indeed Flex is a W-2 staffing agency. The marketing funnel is 7 stages deep (not just "click > apply"), from app download through AI interview, compliance onboarding (I-9, offer letter, pay, legal), to first shift completed. Only **15.4% of accounts** reach a completed first shift — so every stage of the funnel is a marketing lever.

**Scale:**
- **Markets:** 21 US markets across 13 states + DC
- **Clients:** 147 (OnTrac is largest — present in 16+ markets)
- **Channels:** 7 active (Indeed, Google, Meta, TikTok, Bing, Craigslist, Braze CRM)
- **2025 volume:** 52,987 accounts created > 8,146 first shifts completed

## Escalation Protocol

| Level | When | Who Decides | Example |
|-------|------|-------------|---------|
| **L0** | Routine work | Specialist independently | Daily campaign optimization, regular reporting |
| **L1** | Cross-functional or complex | Captain coordinates | Parker needs Fiona's analysis before launching; budget rebalance between channels |
| **L2** | Strategic or budget decisions | Director / VP Marketing | Market expansion, major budget reallocations, new channel adoption |
| **L3** | Company-level impact | Leadership team | Client relationship issues, platform contract changes |

### Escalation Triggers

- Market CR% drops > 5pp in a week
- Spend exceeds budget by > 15%
- Hiring event attendance < 30% of RSVPs
- Channel CPA exceeds 2x benchmark
- Client requests that require new market entry
- Any blocker that stalls work for > 48 hours

## Onboarding Funnel Reference (7 Stages)

The marketing funnel for Indeed Flex is deeper than typical job advertising due to W-2 compliance:

```
Stage 1: Worker Accounts Created     (100.0%)
Stage 2: 1st Role Verified           ( 56.0%)  <-- BIGGEST DROP (44% lost)
Stage 3: 1st OB Task Completed       ( 52.1%)
Stage 4: Platform Verified           ( 36.0%)  <-- 2nd DROP (31% lost)
Stage 5: "Ready to Book" Estimate    ( 28.7%)
Stage 6: 1st Shift Booked            ( 18.2%)  <-- 3rd DROP (37% lost)
Stage 7: 1st Shift Completed         ( 15.4%)
```

### Re-engagement Opportunities at Each Drop-off

| Drop-off Point | People Lost (2025) | Channel | Message |
|----------------|-------------------|---------|---------|
| Account > Verified | 23,294 (44%) | Push + SMS | "Complete your AI interview" |
| OB Task > Platform Verified | 8,523 (31%) | Push + Email | "Finish onboarding — you're almost there" |
| Platform Verified > Ready | 3,867 (20%) | Push | "Complete your last step" |
| Ready > Booked | 5,596 (37%) | Push + SMS | "Shifts available near you now" |
| Booked > Completed | 1,482 (15%) | Push + SMS | "Don't forget your shift tomorrow" |
